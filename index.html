Venues

QWL Stadiums

Six iconic fields across Chappaqua — each with its own character, history, and home-field advantage.

🏠

The Rosey Dome

Capacity: 250 Turf

The crown jewel of QBL venues. The Rosey Dome's covered design means rain never postpones a game.

⚾

Smasher Park

Capacity: 180 Grass

A hitter's paradise. Short fences and firm ground make Smasher Park the home run capital of the QBL.

🌿

The Cheese Common Grounds

Capacity: 150 Grass

The most unique surface in the league. The Cheese Common Grounds rewards patient hitters and disciplined pitching.

🏠

Ant's Shed Stadium

Capacity: 120 Concrete

Built with hustle in a backyard shed setting. Ant's Shed Stadium is intimate, loud, and absolutely electric on game day.

🏗

Tavolacci Development & Co. Field

Capacity: 200 Mixed

Generously named for its chief patron, this sprawling field features championship-grade lines and premium seating.

★

Millers JWB Memorial Field

Capacity: 175 Grass

A field of honor. The Millers JWB Memorial Field carries deep meaning and tradition with every pitch thrown here.
