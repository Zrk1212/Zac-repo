About Mash

Mash is where it all started. As one of the founding franchises of the Quaker Wiffle Ball League, Mash set the tone for what competitive wiffle ball looks like in Chappaqua. No flash, no theatrics — just fundamentally sound, relentless baseball played at its highest plastic form.

The Mash identity is built on consistency and toughness. Every player on this roster has bought into a culture of hard work and accountability. They may not be the loudest team in the QBL, but when Mash steps onto the field, opponents know they're in for a war.

Playing their home games at The Rosey Dome, Mash benefits from the most covered and reliable surface in the league. Rain or shine, Mash plays — and they play to win.
