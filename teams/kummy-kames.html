About Kummy Kames

The Kummy Kames approach every matchup with the intensity of a championship game — because as far as they're concerned, every game is a championship game. This team treats the QBL season as one long audition for the trophy, and their preparation shows in every at-bat.

Kummy Kames is the brains of the QBL. Their pitching strategy is meticulous — they study opposing lineups, catalog tendencies, and attack weaknesses with surgical precision. At the plate, they're patient and calculated, grinding at-bats and waiting for their pitch before unleashing.

Making their home at the prestigious Tavolacci Development & Co. Field gives Kummy Kames a stage that matches their ambitions. The polished venue, firm mixed surface, and 200-person capacity crowd bring out the best in a team that always rises to the moment.
