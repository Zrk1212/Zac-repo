6 Venues • Chappaqua, NY

QBL Stadiums

Six legendary venues. Each one a different character, a different energy, and a different home-field advantage.

🏠
Stadium 01

The Rosey Dome

Capacity: 250 Surface: Turf Type: Covered

The crown jewel of the QBL circuit. The Rosey Dome's covered design is the envy of every other stadium in the league — rain never postpones a game here. The turf surface rewards clean ground balls and sharp fielding, making for a technical, high-IQ brand of wiffle ball.

With the highest capacity of any QBL venue at 250 spectators, the Rosey Dome is the de facto championship venue. The crowd noise inside the dome creates an atmosphere unlike anywhere else in the league.

Stadium 02

Smasher Park

Capacity: 180 Surface: Grass Type: Open-Air

A hitter's paradise through and through. Smasher Park's relatively short fences and firm, flat grass surface are built for power. Home run totals at Smasher Park consistently dwarf every other QBL venue — batters love it, pitchers fear it.

The open-air layout means wind plays a real factor. A well-timed gust can turn a pop-up into a walk-off blast. Every game at Smasher Park is an event — expect offense, expect drama, expect fireworks.

⚾
🌿
Stadium 03

The Cheese Common Grounds

Capacity: 150 Surface: Grass Type: Open-Air

A venue with personality. The Cheese Common Grounds earns its name from a playing surface that plays differently depending on the day — soft after rain, firm under sun, always unique. Disciplined pitchers who can exploit the inconsistency thrive here.

The intimate capacity of 150 creates an up-close viewing experience rarely found in competitive wiffle ball. Regulars at the Cheese feel genuinely connected to the action on every pitch.

Stadium 04

Ant's Shed Stadium

Capacity: 120 Surface: Concrete Type: Semi-Covered

Origin story: built by hustle, sanctioned by passion. Ant's Shed Stadium started as an actual backyard setup and evolved into one of the QBL's most beloved and raucous venues. The concrete surface creates unpredictable bounces that demand quick reflexes from fielders.

The shed backdrop creates a semi-covered environment, and the compact capacity of 120 makes the crowd noise feel massive. Home-field advantage is real at Ant's Shed — visiting teams walk away rattled.

🏠
🏗
Stadium 05

Tavolacci Development & Co. Field

Capacity: 200 Surface: Mixed Type: Open-Air

Generously named for its chief benefactor and patron, Tavolacci Development & Co. Field is the most polished venue in the QBL. Championship-grade chalk lines, premium spectator seating, and a mixed grass-turf surface that plays consistently across all conditions.

With a capacity of 200, it's the second-largest venue in the league. Games here carry extra weight — the corporate naming rights give it a big-league feel that gets competitors' pulses racing from first pitch.

Stadium 06

Millers JWB Memorial Field

Capacity: 175 Surface: Grass Type: Open-Air

The most storied and emotionally significant venue in the QBL. Millers JWB Memorial Field is a field of honor — dedicated to someone whose impact on this community and this game lives on in every play made between these foul lines.

Playing at the Memorial Field carries weight. The lush grass surface, the 175-person capacity, and the quiet reverence players bring to this venue make it unlike any other in the league. Respect the field. Honor the name.
